RFM Revenue Analysis:

This project analyzes 500K+ e-commerce transactions to identify high-value customers at risk of churn using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) segmentation.

The analysis reveals:
- ₹4.58L revenue at risk
- 5.14% of total revenue exposed to churn risk
- At-risk customers have 2.3x higher return rates compared to loyal customers

Business Problem
High-value customers contribute a significant portion of overall revenue. If these customers churn, the business faces substantial revenue loss. This project identifies such customers and quantifies potential revenue exposure.

Methodology:-
Data Cleaning
Removed cancelled transactions
Handled missing values
Converted date fields

RFM Calculation
- Recency: Days since last purchase
- Frequency: Number of purchases
- Monetary: Total spending

Customer Segmentation
Customers were grouped into:
Champions, General Customers, At-Risk High-Value Customers

Revenue at Risk Analysis:

- Total Revenue: ₹89.11L
- Revenue At Risk: ₹4.58L
- Percentage At Risk: 5.14%

Return Behavior Analysis
At-risk customers show significantly higher return behavior, indicating potential dissatisfaction and churn risk.

Key Insights:

A small customer segment contributes disproportionately to revenue risk.
Return behavior can act as an early churn signal.
Targeted retention strategies can protect high-value revenue.
Business Recommendations
Personalized retention campaigns
Loyalty rewards for high-value customers
Analysis of return reasons to reduce dissatisfaction
